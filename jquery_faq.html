4 Facts About Big Bend National Park

  • Big Bend National Park covers a total area of 801,163 acres
  • The number of people visiting Big Bend in 2019 was 463,832
  • Big Bend was made a national park on June 20, 1935
  • The lowest elevation found in Big Bend is 1,715 feet at the Rio Grande River

4 Facts About Guadalupe Mountains National Park

  • Guadalupe Mountains National Park covers a total area of 86,416 acres
  • The number of people visiting Guadalupe Mountains in 2019 was 188,833
  • Guadalupe Mountains was made a national park on October 15, 1966
  • The lowest elevation found in Guadalupe Mountains is 3,636 feet near Williams Road
